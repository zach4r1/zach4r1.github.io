Problem statement: Walkable Cities

The city of Columbia is not a walkable city for pedestrians due to the lack of accessible sidewalks/crosswalks and continued construction.

Affinity diagram: Our Unwalkable City

The affinity diagram explains problems, potential solutions, constants, and potential ideas that can fix the issue of our unwalkable city. Cluster 1: Physically fixable issues Cluster 2: Constants that can never be "fixed" Cluster 3: Fixes for potential issues that cause the City of Columbia to be hard to walk in Cluster 4: Potential solutions for this problem via adding new things. Cluster 5: Potential inventions that can remedy issues with constants (e.g, weather, traffic, construction)
